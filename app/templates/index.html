Welcome to My Educational Chatbot! (CliffsNotes Variant)

This chatbot is designed to answer questions based on a given text. Here's how it works:

  • Click the button below to upload the text you want to use.
  • Once the text is uploaded, you will be redirected to the chatbot screen.
  • There will be a prompt for you to ask your questions to the chatbot.
